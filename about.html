About Me
Growing up in Washington has given me nothing but
opportunity to explore. I've taken every chance I've been
presented to take the roads less traveled and make my own
path. My parents have always pushed me to be creative and never quit when faced with a problem. Thankfully I had one of the best outlets a kid could ever want, a garage with way too
many tools. I started at a young age tearing electronics apart,
building portable charging systems, and inventing items that
had no purpose. But with each pointless invention I continued
to gain experience and knowledge. Now, in my mid twenties,
nothing has changed but the complexity of my projects. I'm
always creating in my mini shop and always looking for new
challenges and opportunities.
